How to Apply
Your Simple Routine,
Visible Results
Step 01
Cleanse Your Skin
Start with a gentle cleanser on damp skin. Pat dry with a soft towel — never rub.
Step 02
Apply the Cream
Take a pea-sized amount and warm it between your fingertips. Gently press onto face and neck.
Step 03
Let It Absorb
Allow the cream to fully absorb before applying sunscreen. Use morning and evening for best results.
