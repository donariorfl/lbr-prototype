Usage Guide

How to Apply

STEP 01
Cleanse Thoroughly
Begin with a gentle, pH-balanced cleanser to remove impurities and prepare skin for optimal serum absorption.
STEP 02
Apply 2–3 Drops
Dispense 2–3 drops onto your fingertips and gently warm the serum. Apply evenly across face and neck.
STEP 03
Pat Until Absorbed
Gently pat — do not rub — until fully absorbed. Follow with your moisturizer to lock in active ingredients.
